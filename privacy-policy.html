Privacy Policy

Telegram Reminder respects your privacy.

This app collects only the minimum data necessary to function correctly. The only data collected is the user's Telegram ID, which is used exclusively to associate reminders with the correct Telegram account.

No personal data is sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

Data is stored securely and used only to provide app functionality. Users may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting support.

This app does not collect sensitive personal data, financial data, health data, or precise location data.

Contact

Email: support@telegram-reminder.app

Data Deletion

Users can request the deletion of their personal data at any time by:

  • Sending a command to the Telegram bot (e.g. /delete or /stop)
  • Or by contacting us at: axel.reminder.bot@gmail.com

All associated data (Telegram ID, reminders, subscription status) will be permanently deleted.
